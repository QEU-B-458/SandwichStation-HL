# Security Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

## Reporting Channel
Security vulnerabilities must be reported exclusively via email to **fax@sandwich14.com**. Reports submitted through other channels (issue trackers, public forums, social media, etc.) will not be accepted and may be deleted without review.

## Responsible Disclosure Requirements
By submitting a vulnerability report, you agree to the following terms:

1. **Non-Disclosure**: You shall not publicly disclose, discuss, or share details of the reported vulnerability—including with third parties—until explicit written authorization is granted by SandwichStation.

2. **Good Faith Research**: Your research and testing must be conducted in good faith, without causing harm to systems, data, or users. Unauthorized access, exploitation, or disruption of services is strictly prohibited.

3. **Information Required**: To facilitate timely review, please include:
   - Description of the vulnerability
   - Steps to reproduce the issue
   - Potential impact assessment
   - Your contact information
   - Date of discovery

## Review Process
- We will acknowledge receipt of your report within **7 business days**.
- We will provide status updates at reasonable intervals during investigation.
- Estimated time to resolution varies based on severity and complexity.

## Safe Harbor
We commit to not pursuing legal action against researchers who report vulnerabilities in accordance with this policy, provided their actions remain within the scope of responsible disclosure and do not involve data exfiltration, system damage, or unauthorized access beyond what is necessary to demonstrate the vulnerability.

## Exceptions
This policy does not apply to vulnerabilities disclosed by third parties prior to your report, or vulnerabilities already known to us through other channels.
